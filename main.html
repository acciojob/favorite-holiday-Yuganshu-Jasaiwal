Kashmir


Kashmir is a region in the northwestern Indian subcontinent, covering around 86,000 sq miles (222,200 sq km), and known for its lakes, meadows and mountains123. It is bounded by China, India, Pakistan, and Afghanistan23. It was a princely state that was free to join either India or Pakistan after the British partition in 194714. However, both India and Pakistan claimed the whole region, leading to a territorial conflict that resulted in three wars and several skirmishes
